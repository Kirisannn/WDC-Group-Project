FAQ

Accounts


Q: What do I do if I forget my password?
A: Go into login and click on 'Forgot Password' then enter your email to receive a notification to change your password.

Club Managers


Q: How do I get in contact with a club manager?
A: Club managers details should be available in the selected club page, use those details to get into contact with them.

Clubs


Q: How do I join a club?
A: Send in a request to join the club, club managers should be notified of this and send you an email regarding instructions on how to join.
